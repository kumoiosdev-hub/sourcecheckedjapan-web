Privacy

Last updated: 2026-07-20.

This notice explains what Source-checked Japan collects, why, how long it is kept, and your choices.

Who is responsible

Source-checked Japan is an independent travel-information project operated in Japan under the site name Source-checked Japan. For privacy, access, deletion, correction, or withdrawal requests, use the contact form below. We respond within a reasonable time, normally within 14 days.

What we collect and why

ActivityDataPurposeRetention
Site and CTA measurement Page path viewed, and CTA display/click events, sent to a measurement endpoint we operate on serverless infrastructure (Twilio). No cookies, no advertising trackers, no third-party analytics services, no persistent identifiers. Email addresses and form contents are never sent to measurement counters. Privacy-minimised aggregate measurement of what is useful. Aggregate counters up to 12 months.
Yuasa map interest test A purchase-interest click event; your email only if you separately submit it with consent. Test demand for a proposed US$7 map. The interest test is not a purchase and no payment details are requested. Email until unsubscribe, the decision to release or not release the map, or 6 months after signup, whichever comes first.
Mt.FUJI100 2027 alert Email, signup source and version, and your consent record. Send the requested official-update alerts, based on consent. This is an independent alert, not an official race-organizer service. Until unsubscribe or 90 days after the 2027 event.
Privacy / correction contact Your message; your email only if you include it for a reply. Respond to your request and fix errors. Kept with the request log; deleted on request once resolved.

Submitted emails are stored on infrastructure we control (Twilio Sync, processed under Twilio's data processing terms) and are not shared with, or sold to, any third party.

What we do not do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not require an account to read articles.
  • We do not use cookies or cross-site tracking.
  • We do not place secrets, tokens, or payment details in URLs, forms, or logs.

Contact / unsubscribe

Use this form for privacy questions, unsubscribe requests, data deletion, and correction reports. Include the article URL if you are reporting an error. Do not send passwords, identity documents, or other sensitive information.
