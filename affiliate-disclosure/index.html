Affiliate disclosure

Some links on Source-checked Japan — for example, links to book lodging, tours, or transport — may be affiliate links. If you book through one of these links, we may earn a small commission at no extra cost to you.

What this means for you

  • We do not take bookings directly, and we do not hold or process payment on your behalf.
  • An affiliate relationship never determines whether a place is included in an article, or whether a claim about it is marked verified.
  • Where an article has an affiliate link, this disclosure is shown before the link, not buried at the bottom of the page.

Status in this build

This is a local, unpublished build. No live affiliate program has been connected, and no article in this build has cleared its publish gate. Booking links you see are placeholders showing where the disclosure and link will appear once an article is verified and approved for publication.
